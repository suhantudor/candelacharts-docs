# Divergences

Divergences identify the subtle mismatch between Price and Momentum, often signaling an upcoming trend reversal.

<figure><img src="../../.gitbook/assets/docs-momentum-concepts-004 (1).png" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

### Detection Logic

The indicator automatically scans for **Regular Divergences** using the internal Momentum Wave (MW1) values:

1. **Regular Bullish Divergence**:
   * Price makes a **Lower Low**.
   * Momentum Wave makes a **Higher Low**.
   * Indicates that selling pressure is weakening even though price is falling.
2. **Regular Bearish Divergence**:
   * Price makes a **Higher High**.
   * Momentum Wave makes a **Lower High**.
   * Indicates that buying pressure is exhausting even though price is rising.

### Visual Clues

* **Green Line**: A bullish divergence has been detected between two pivot points.
* **Orange Line**: A bearish divergence has been detected between two pivot points.

### Settings

You can adjust the **Pivot Length** and **Range** in the settings to filter for long-term or short-term divergences. Longer ranges typically provide more significant signals.
