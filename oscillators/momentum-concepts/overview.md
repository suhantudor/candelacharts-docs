# Overview

**Momentum Concepts** is an all-in-one momentum intelligence dashboard designed to help traders navigate the complexities of modern markets. Instead of relying on a single signal, it provides a 360-degree view of market conviction by combining trend analysis, capital flow tracking, and predictive probability modeling.
