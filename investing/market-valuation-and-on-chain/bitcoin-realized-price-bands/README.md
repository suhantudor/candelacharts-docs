# Bitcoin Realized Price Bands

The **CandelaCharts - Bitcoin Realized Price Bands** indicator maps out key valuation zones for Bitcoin by comparing its current price against its Realized Price.&#x20;

By defining multiple bands above and below the Realized Price, this tool helps long-term investors and macro traders identify periods of extreme undervaluation (buying opportunities) and extreme overvaluation (taking profits).
