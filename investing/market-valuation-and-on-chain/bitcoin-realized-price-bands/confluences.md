# Confluences

The Bitcoin Realized Price Bands are a macro fundamental tool and should be paired with technical momentum indicators for precise timing:

* **Trend Indicators**: Wait for a momentum indicator (like MACD or a moving average crossover) to turn bullish _after_ Bitcoin dips into the Undervalued zone.
* **Liquidity & Macro**: Pair this indicator with the Macro Liquidity Gap. If global liquidity is expanding while Bitcoin is sitting near its Realized Price, the setup offers tremendous asymmetric upside.
