# Sector Rotation Map 🔜

The Sector Rotation Map is a sophisticated analytical framework designed to help traders and investors navigate the complexities of the US stock market by tracking the flow of institutional capital.

&#x20;By monitoring twelve distinct sector ETFs (including Tech, Energy, Financials, and Staples), this tool provides a real-time visualization of market leadership and weakness.&#x20;

Understanding where "smart money" is moving allows you to align your investment strategy with the four key phases of the economic cycle—Early Recovery, Expansion, Late Cycle, and Recession—ensuring your portfolio remains positioned in high-conviction segments while avoiding laggards.
