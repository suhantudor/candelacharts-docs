# Features

This tool brings institutional-level valuation data to your charts with several key features for deep historical analysis.

* **Official Data Integration**: Pulls accurate historical CAPE10 data directly onto your charts (Monthly or Yearly resolution).
* **Valuation Zones**: Automatically classifies the market into five regimes: **Extreme Undervalued**, **Undervalued**, **Fair Value**, **Overvalued**, and **Extreme Overvalued**.
* **Historical Mean Tracking**: Includes a customizable reference line (default \~17.0) to measure the current market's deviation from its long-term average.
* **Visual Sentiment Heatmap**: A gradient-based line and optional background pulses highlight periods of extreme market "froth" or generational value.
* **Performance Dashboard**: Displays real-time data including current value, historical mean, percentage deviation, and current zone classification.
