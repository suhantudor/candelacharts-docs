# Features

This tool provides a comprehensive set of features designed to improve your analysis and decision-making process.&#x20;

Key capabilities include:

* **Dual UI Modes**: Choose between a comprehensive **Heatmap Table** to see multiple years at a glance, or a focused **Bar Chart** for a detailed view of a specific year's monthly returns.
* **YTD Calculations**: The Heatmap Table automatically calculates and displays both Geometric (Geo YTD) and Arithmetic (Ari YTD) year-to-date performance.
* **Deep Historical Lookback**: Visualize up to 30 years of historical monthly data directly on your chart, allowing for robust statistical observation.
* **Dynamic Styling**: Fully customizable colors for bullish and bearish returns with dynamic gradient shading based on the magnitude of the returns. The darker the color, the more extreme the return.
