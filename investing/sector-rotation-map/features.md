# Features

The Sector Rotation Map is packed with professional-grade features designed to give you a comprehensive view of market dynamics.

* **Multi-Sector Tracking**: Simultaneously monitors 12 key sectors including Technology (XLK), Energy (XLE), Financials (XLF), Health Care (XLV), and more.
* **Rotation Classification**: Automatically categorizes each sector into one of four phases: **Leading**, **Weakening**, **Lagging**, or **Improving** based on Relative Strength momentum.
* **Economic Cycle Mapping**: Anchors sectors to their typical outperformance phases (Early Recovery, Expansion, Late Cycle, Recession).
* **Interactive Dashboard**: Features a sortable table showing Percentage Change, Relative Strength, Relative Volume, and Current Rotation Phase.
* **Smart Visuals**: Includes line plots for visual trend analysis and interactive tooltips that explain the fundamental drivers of each sector.
