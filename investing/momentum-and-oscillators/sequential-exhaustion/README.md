# Sequential Exhaustion

Sequential Exhaustion is not just another oscillator. It is a precision instrument designed for traders who demand clarity in the face of market volatility. By shifting the focus from closing prices to the actual "exhaustion" of demand at price extremes, this tool identifies high-probability reversal points before they become obvious to the rest of the market.

In modern markets, traditional indicators often lag or provide false signals during "blow-off tops" or "climax bottoms."&#x20;

Sequential Exhaustion solves this by:

* **Filtering Noise**: Focusing on price action at the margins.
* **Visualizing Pressure**: Using the Momentum Radar to show exactly when a move is overextended.
* **Validating Reversals**: Providing a signal hierarchy that distinguishes between minor pullbacks and major trend changes.
