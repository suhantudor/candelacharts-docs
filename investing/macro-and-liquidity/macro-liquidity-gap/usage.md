# Usage

Finding deep value "Buy Zones" relies on understanding the divergence between central bank liquidity and asset prices.&#x20;

Here is how to interpret the divergence lines and actionable signals:

* **Identifying Value**: When the Divergence line drops below the Buy Level threshold, the indicator paints a green **Buy Zone**. This implies that the selected index has fallen much further than the underlying global money supply supports, highlighting a macroeconomic value area.
* **Catching Bottoms**: Watch for the **Buy Signal** triangles on the main chart. These appear exactly when the market exits the deep Buy Zone, historically signaling the end of a liquidity-driven correction and the beginning of a fresh uptrend.
* **Macro Context**: Use the divergence line to gauge the broader environment. A rising divergence line means liquidity is expanding faster than the market index, which provides a bullish tailwind.
